Agripur by Ostraci Innovations

Agripur Pesticide Remover, awarded the IUFoST (International Union of Food Science and Technology) award in 2018, is designed to eliminate 99.99% of pesticides and chemicals from vegetables and fruits.

Available in 1.5-gram sachets, dissolve these in 2 liters of water to soak vegetables, fruits, fish, or meat for 20 minutes. Any present pesticides or chemicals can be observed as powders or streaks on the solution's surface.

  • The use of pesticides and chemicals is inevitable in food production and storage.
  • These substances pose significant health hazards to humans, often applied to food surfaces.
  • Regular washing doesn't effectively remove these toxins from vegetables and fruits.

Agripur Pesticide Remover has become a vital tool for industrial producers, helping them avoid pesticide-related rejections in exports. As global food production rises, the use of pesticides is expected to increase, making Agripur an essential solution to neutralize these toxins.
